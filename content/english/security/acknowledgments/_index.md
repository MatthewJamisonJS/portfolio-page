This page exists so the `Acknowledgments:` field in [/.well-known/security.txt](/.well-known/security.txt) resolves to a real URL per [RFC 9116](https://www.rfc-editor.org/rfc/rfc9116). It is intentionally `noindex` until the first acknowledgment lands.

## Reporters

No public acknowledgments yet. If you have responsibly disclosed a security finding under our [security policy](https://github.com/MatthewJamisonJS/portfolio-page/blob/main/SECURITY.md) and would like to be credited here with your consent, please mention this in your report.

## What gets listed here

With the reporter's consent:

- Reporter's display name (real name or chosen handle)
- Optional link (personal site, GitHub, LinkedIn, X)
- Category of finding (no PoCs or exploit details)
- Date of report

## What does not get listed

- Reports we couldn't reproduce
- Reports outside the [scope](https://github.com/MatthewJamisonJS/portfolio-page/blob/main/SECURITY.md#scope) of our security policy
- Anything containing third-party PII

## Reporting a new finding

See [SECURITY.md](https://github.com/MatthewJamisonJS/portfolio-page/blob/main/SECURITY.md) for the disclosure policy, in-scope/out-of-scope surface, safe-harbor commitment, and response timeline.
